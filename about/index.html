About The Friendly Snail

Our Story

It all started with a snail and a cucumber slice. The Friendly Snail was born from a passion for providing the best veggie snacks for aquarium pets — from crunchy carrot plates to delicious snail burgers. Our goal is to make aquatic life tastier, healthier, and happier.

Our Mission

We’re dedicated to crafting wholesome, eco-friendly snacks for freshwater snails, shrimp, and herbivorous fish. All ingredients are selected with care and love, because every little creature deserves a gourmet experience.
