Description
Made from a vibrant swirl of fresh spinach, cucumber, broccoli, and carrot — with a touch of calcium carbonate and nutrient-rich Limnospira — our Spinach Snail Wraps are the ultimate slow-snack for your aquatic friends. Carefully crafted into soft, bite-sized spirals, they support shell health, digestion, and enrichment, all while keeping your tank pristine.

✅ 100% vegetarian
✅ Snail-safe & aquarium-friendly
✅ No added preservatives

Let your snails savour every bite — because healthy snacking never looked so green!

Directions for Use
Get a small, shallow container and fill it with some water from the aquarium
Gently push on the plastic of the blister pack to push the spinach wrap out
Put the spinach wrap in the shallow container and let sit for 5-10 minutes to allow for full thawing
Once the wrap is fully thawed, spear it on a weighted skewer or another utensil
Gently place it in the tank and pour the water in with it
Let your snails enjoy their brand new snack!

One Spinach Wrap should last 2 average-sized mystery snails at least 2 days
Storage Instructions
Store in a cool, dry place.
Freeze after opening for maximum freshness.
Use within 6 months of opening.
